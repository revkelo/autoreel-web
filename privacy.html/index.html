Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

1. Overview

Autoreel ("the Service") is a personal, single-user content automation tool operated by its sole owner ("the Owner"). This policy describes what data the Service and this website handle. In short: the Service does not collect, sell or share personal data from the public, because it has no public users.

2. Data Collected by This Website

This website is a static informational page. It does not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels or contact forms, and it does not collect any personal information from visitors.

3. Data Used by the Service

  • Platform credentials: The Service stores OAuth access and refresh tokens for the Owner's own TikTok and YouTube accounts. These tokens are stored privately on the Owner's own infrastructure and are used solely to upload the Owner's videos.
  • Basic account information: Through the user.info.basic scope, the Service may read the Owner's own basic profile information (such as display name and avatar) to confirm the connected account.
  • Video content and metrics: The Service uploads videos created by the Owner and may read engagement statistics (views, likes, comments) of the Owner's own published videos to inform content decisions.

4. What the Service Does NOT Do

  • It does not access, collect or process data from any third-party or public TikTok/YouTube users.
  • It does not sell, rent or share any data with third parties.
  • It does not use data for advertising or profiling.

5. Data Storage and Security

Credentials and video metadata are stored in a private database on infrastructure controlled by the Owner. Access is restricted to the Owner. Tokens can be revoked at any time from the connected platform's security settings, which immediately disables the Service's access.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only while the Service is in use. The Owner may delete stored tokens, videos and metrics at any time. Revoking the app's access from TikTok or Google account settings also terminates any further data access.

7. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with the TikTok Content Posting API and the YouTube Data API under their respective terms and privacy policies. Please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for how those platforms process data.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
